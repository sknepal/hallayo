About


A simple app that helps you post to Facebook or Twitter a message (that you specify) whenever you feel an Earthquake and/or it's aftershock.

Made in response to this tweet. I don't expect anyone to use it; I just took the tweet too literally. The Facebook share does not seem to work at times (or all the time.)


Version: 0.1.

Developed by @SkNepal during Nepal Earthquake 2015.

Published on: May 15, 2015.
